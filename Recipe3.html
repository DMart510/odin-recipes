Ingredients

  • 2 Small cans of black chopped olives
  • 3-4 green onions finely chopped
  • 8oz bag of Medium shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 8oz bag of medium shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2-3 Tbsp of Mayonnaise
  • 1/2 Tsp Garlic salt or season to taste

Steps

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a medium size bowl
  2. Refrigerate for a few hours and/or overnight
  3. Preheat oven to 375F or broil (*Just make sure to keep and eye on them) :-)
  4. Place sliced baguettes on the cookie sheet lined w/ foil
  5. Spread pizza mixture onto the baguettes
  6. Bake for 10 minutes or until the spread has melted
  7. Cool before eating
  8. Bon Appetit!
