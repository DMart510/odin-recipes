Ingredients

  • 1lb - Chicken Wings and/or drumsticks
  • 1/2 Tsp Mrs. Dash
  • 1/2 Tsp Granulated Garlic Powder or season to taste
  • 1/2 Tsp Tony Chachere's Original Creole Seasoning or season to taste
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Wash/Rinse chicken off in sink.
  2. Pat and dry off with napkin
  3. Add Mrs. Dash seasoning to chicken
  4. Add Tony Chachere's Original Creole Seasoning to the chicken
  5. Add Granulated Garlic Powder to the chicken
  6. Add Parsley for presentation
